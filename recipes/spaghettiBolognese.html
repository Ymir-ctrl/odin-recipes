Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

It consists of spaghetti served with a sauce made from tomatoes, minced beef, garlic, wine and herbs; sometimes minced beef can be replaced by other minced meats.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato sauce
  • Minced meat
  • Garlic cloves
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Cook noodles
  2. Cut onions and garlic
  3. Fry onions and garlic in a hot pan
  4. After 3 minutes, add minced meat
  5. Add tomato sauce and cook for a few minutes
