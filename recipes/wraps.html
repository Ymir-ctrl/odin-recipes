Wraps

Description

A wrap is a culinary dish made with a soft flatbread rolled around a filling.

The usual flatbreads are wheat tortillas, lavash, or pita; the filling may include cold sliced meat, poultry, or fish, shredded lettuce, diced tomato or pico de gallo, guacamole, sauteed mushrooms, bacon, grilled onions, cheese, and a sauce, such as ranch or honey mustard.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Avocado
  • Minced meat
  • Sriracha
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber

Steps

  1. Fry minced meat
  2. Put minced meat and vegetables in the middle of the tortilla
  3. Roll the tortilla
